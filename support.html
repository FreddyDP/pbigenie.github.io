Support Policy

Last updated: 29 December 2025

Support channels

  • Email: support@pbigenie.com

Support hours

Standard support is provided on business days, 9:00–17:00 (AWST, UTC+8), excluding public holidays in Western Australia.

Response targets

  • Critical (visual fails to render, blocks reporting): initial response within 1 business day
  • High (major feature broken, severe usability issue): 2 business days
  • Normal (general questions, minor defects): 5 business days

What’s included

  • Installation and basic usage guidance
  • Bug triage and fixes (where reproducible)
  • Compatibility guidance for supported Power BI versions

What’s not included

  • Custom DAX, report redesign, or bespoke consulting services
  • Debugging issues caused by third-party visuals, unsupported browsers, or non-standard environments
  • Urgent after-hours support (unless separately agreed)

How to report an issue

Please include:

  • Power BI version and platform (Desktop / Service)
  • Visual version (from the visual’s About section, if available)
  • Steps to reproduce and expected vs actual behavior
  • Screenshots and/or a small PBIX with sample data (if possible)

Updates

PBIGenie may release updates to improve performance, add features, or address defects. Some updates may be required to maintain compatibility with Power BI platform changes.
